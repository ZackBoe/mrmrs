Principles of Design

Good design starts with problem-finding and problem-shaping.

Things should be as fast as possible.

Words are important and should be treated with time and care.

Everything should work.

Everything should be highly readable.

The user should be able to know where they are and how to get where they want at all times.

Doing simple things well takes a lot of time and effort. But it's worth it.

Design from the gut. But also design systems that gather information and help you make increasingly informed decisions over time.

The user should always come first.

At the end of the day most apps are just a bunch of code. If it's a web app it's just a bunch of html and css and javascript. That's what you send to your users. It's what renders to their screen. And I think every detail of how it works matters. I think that's where your real design is.
